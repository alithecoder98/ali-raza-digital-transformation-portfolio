Systems thinking
meets strategic delivery

I started in Telecom Engineering — supervising microwave deployments, managing field teams across Pakistan's North Region, and learning firsthand how infrastructure actually fails (and how to keep it from doing so).

That systems mindset carried me into Project Management at Blutech Consulting, where I coordinated digital transformation for HBL, UBL, and RawBank — building Power BI dashboards, automating incident pipelines, and turning chaos into KPIs.

Now I'm building in AI and data engineering — constructing RAG pipelines, agentic workflows, and LLM-powered products. Not as a pivot, but as a natural extension of understanding systems end-to-end.

I understand both the strategy layer (why we build) and the systems layer (how it runs). That intersection is where I do my best work.
